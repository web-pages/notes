Weeks 1 to 3

Day 1

  1. Squats 5x15,12,10,8,6
  2. Bent Over Rows 5x12,10,8,6,6
  3. Bench Press 5x15,12,10,8,6
  4. Overhead Press 5x12,10,8,6,6
  5. External Rotations 3x12*
  6. Seated Calf Raises 3x15
  7. Mountain Climbers 3x30 seconds
  8. Planks 3x30 seconds

Day 2

  1. Deadlift 5x15,12,10,8,6
  2. Kneeling Landmine Press 5x15,12,10,8,8
  3. Alt. Arnold Press 5x12,10,8,8,6
  4. Alt. Front Lunge 5x10
  5. Dumbbell Pullovers 3x15
  6. Weighted Crunches 3x15 (feet on wall)
  7. Side Planks 3x30 seconds

Day 3

  1. Front Squat 5x15,12,10,8,6
  2. T-Bar Row 5x12,10,8,8,6
  3. Dips 5x15
  4. Upright Row 5x15,12,10,8,8
  5. Glute Bridges (weighted) 3x10
  6. Standing Calf Raises 3x15
  7. Russian Twists 3x30 (15 each side)
