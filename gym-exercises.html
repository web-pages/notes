Lower Body

Deadlift

To Begin

  1. Feet slightly wider than hip width, toes pointing out.
  2. Hands just outside legs.
  3. Shoulder blades directly over the bar.

Movement

  1. Keep chest high.
  2. Keep hips out.
  3. Back erect with very slight arch.
  4. Bar must roll up the shins and stay in line with legs.
  5. When reaching mid-way point of lift, push hips outward.
  6. Hold for two seconds.
  7. Return bar to gound, pushing hips inwards, and slightly bending the knees.

Avoid This

  1. Do not arch the back by letting the chest drop and shoulders go slack.
  2. Hips must always be higher than your knee - never let it drop below the knee.
  3. Do not lean backwards or forwards throughout the movement - keep it straight.

Front Squat

To Begin

  1. Feet slightly wider than shoulder width (so there is space to squat)

Movement

  1. Keep chest up.
  2. Keep core tight.
  3. Squat to at least parallel to the floor (can go below parallel, as long as chest is up)

Squat

To Begin

  1. Feet: Shoulder width apart.
  2. Toes: at a comfortable outwards pointing position.
  3. Rest Bar on upper shoulders

Movement

  1. Keep chest up.
  2. Lower body as if going to sit in a small chair.
  3. Stop when hips are lower than knees.
  4. While putting pressure on the heels, lead with chest while using glutes to rise.

Avoid This

  1. Heels: must be firmly on the ground throughout the movement
  2. Knees: make sure they don't track inwards.
  3. Do not place bar at the base of kneck – keep it at the upper trap

Upper Body

Arnold Press

To Begin

  1. Place weights on your upper legs - pump up legs to start with weights.
  2. Start with palms facing in, drop elbows so dumbbells are facing chest.

Movement

  1. Push dumbbells apart, rotating arms forward.

Avoid This

  1. Arms leaning forward - keep forearms upright.
  2. Focus all effort on shoulder, do not make a big wrist movement.

Landmine Chest Press

To Begin

  1. Start on knees, about shoulder width apart.
  2. Grab barbell right at the end, clasp both hands together to make a solid base.
  3. Bring it right below the chin.
  4. Lean slightly towards the bar.
  5. Keep the back straight.
  6. Chest up, shoulders tucked in together, and elbows tucked in.

Movement

  1. Press to get the full extension of the arms, sqeezing chest the entire way.
  2. Bring bar down slowly.
