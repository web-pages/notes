The primal blueprint: What Food To Avoid

Avoid All Grains

  • Grains offer a great majority of their calories in the form of carbs
  • Most grains contain phytates that bind to important minerals making them more difficult to abosrb
    • calcium, magnesium and zinc are examples of such minerals.
  • Some grains contain gluten, which tends to trigger an auto-immune response due to it closely mimicing viruses.
  • Grains contain high levels of mild plant toxins called lectins.

Grains To Avoid

  1. Wheat
  2. Rice
  3. Oats
  4. Cornmeal
  5. Barley

Avoid Polyunsaturated Fats (PUFAs)

PUFAs To Avoid

  1. Canola Oil
  2. Margerine
  3. Corn, safflower, sunflower, soybean and cottonsead oil
  4. Vegetable shortening

Avoid Trans And Partially Hydrogenated Fats

Partially Hydrogenated, Hydrogenated and Trans fats are evil. Avoid at all costs. They are found in most processed foods, including:

Trans and Hydrogenated Fats To Avoid

  1. Frozen dinners
  2. Candy
  3. Packaged snacks (crackers, chips and cookies)
  4. Peanut butter

Avoid Legumes

Legumes have significant levels of carbs. Most legumes are non-digestible/toxic to mammals, and need to be altered (cooked) for consumption.

Legumes To Avoid

  1. Alfalfa
  2. Beans
  3. Lentils
  4. Peanuts
  5. Peas
  6. Soy products
