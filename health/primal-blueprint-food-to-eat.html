Primal Blueprint Eating Pyramid

Meat, Fowl and Fish

Fish

Fish are a rich source of Omega-3 fatty acids

Farmed Fish

Farmed Fish To Eat

  1. Fresh water coho Salmon
  2. Barramudi, catfish, crayfish and tilapia.
  3. Trout farmed in Canada or USA.
  4. Shell fish

Avoid farmed Atlantic salmon.

Vegetables

Vegetables To Eat

  1. Avocado
  2. Beets
  3. Broccoli
  4. Brussels Sprouts
  5. Carrots
  6. Cauliflower
  7. Eggplant
  8. Garlic
  9. Kale
  10. Onion
  11. Red bell pepper
  12. Spinach
  13. Yellow squash

Coconut Products

Use Coconut Products

  1. Coconut oil
  2. Coconut milk
  3. Coconut butter
  4. Coconut water
  5. Cocnut flakes

Medium Fatty Acids

Coconuts are an excellent source of medium fatty acids (very difficult to find in other foods)

  • Offers protection against heart disease, cancer, diabetes extract

Snacks

Farmed Fish To Eat

  1. Biltong
  2. Celery (enjoy with some cream cheese)
  3. Cottage cheese (enjoy with nuts, berries, balsamic vinegar etc)
  4. Dark chocolate
  5. Fish (canned tuna)
  6. Fresh berries (raspberries, blueberries, blackberries, strawberries)
  7. Hard-boiled eggs (sprinkle some salt and olive oil)
  8. Macadamia nuts
  9. Olives

Fruits

Some fruits should be eaten in moderation due to high fructose. Look for fruits that:

  1. High in anti-oxidants
  2. Have a low Total Metabolic FructoseL11

Note: Avoid fruit juice - it is high in sugar, and low in macro nutrients

Best Fruits To Eat

These fruits are high anti-oxidant, low glycemic, low TMF

  1. All berries (strawberries, blueberries, blackberries etc)
  2. Most pitted fruits (cherries, peaches, apricots)
  3. Avocados
  4. Casaba melon
  5. Lime and Lemon
  6. Tomato
  7. Guava

Good Fruits To Eat

These fruits are lower anti-oxidant, higher glycemic, medium TMF

  1. Apples
  2. Bananas
  3. Cantaloupe
  4. Grapefruit
  5. Kiwi
  6. Pomegranate

Moderate These Fruits

These fruits are low anti-oxidant, high glycemic, higher TMF

  1. Dates
  2. Dried fruits
  3. Grapes (all types of grapes)
  4. Mangoes
  5. Melons
  6. Nectarines
  7. Oranges
  8. Papayas
  9. Pineapples
  10. Plums
  11. Tangerines

Herbs And Spices

These are great - eat as much as you want

Nuts And Seeds

Should be ground up and added to salads etc - but don't overdo it!
